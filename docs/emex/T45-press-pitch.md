## Subject line — A/B options

Pick one per recipient, both under 60 chars:

- **A.** *Five NZ manufacturers, $1M ARR, an AI partnership that flips SaaS* (60)
- **B.** *Quintinity at EMEX 2026 — auditable AI for NZ manufacturing* (58)

Default to A for business desks (NBR, BusinessDesk, Idealog, The Post). Default to B for trade and sector outlets (NZ Manufacturer, Manufacturers' Network, EMA).
